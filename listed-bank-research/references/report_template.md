### B. Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| NIM | Net Interest Margin — net interest income as a percentage of average interest-earning assets |
| NPL | Non-Performing Loan — loan classified as substandard, doubtful, or loss |
| CAR | Capital Adequacy Ratio — total capital divided by risk-weighted assets |
| CET1 | Common Equity Tier 1 — highest quality regulatory capital |
| LCR | Liquidity Coverage Ratio — high-quality liquid assets divided by 30-day net cash outflows |
| NSFR | Net Stable Funding Ratio — available stable funding divided by required stable funding |
| LDR | Loan-to-Deposit Ratio — total loans divided by total deposits |
| LGFV | Local Government Financing Vehicle — entities used by local governments to raise funds |
| RWA | Risk-Weighted Assets — total assets adjusted for risk weighting |
| LTV | Loan-to-Value Ratio — loan amount as a percentage of collateral value |
| D-SIB | Domestic Systemically Important Bank |
| bps | Basis points (1 bp = 0.01%) |
